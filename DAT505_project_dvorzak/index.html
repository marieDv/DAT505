Angèle

Angèle Van Laeken is a Belgian singer and songwriter with a classical background. She had an early break through with only 22 but is already well known due to her unique humorful lyrics and the colorful pop rhythms in her songs. She finds her inspiration in various types of music, from French chanson to electronic music and rap over to songs from one of the artist that most moved her Ella Fitzgerald.
